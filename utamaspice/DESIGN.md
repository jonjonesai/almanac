# Utama Spice — SP2 Video Visual Identity

## Style Prompt

Artisan soap from Bali. Plant-rooted, sensory, story-driven. Terracotta accents on warm earth-brown canvas. Slow breath-paced motion (1-2s entrances, never fast slams). Serif headlines with personality carry the craft feel; clean bold sans body keeps captions readable on video. Warmth + restraint. This is a brand for someone who cares where their soap comes from, who made it, and what plant it started as.

## Colors

| Hex       | Role                                                                  |
| --------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| `#c66b3f` | Primary — terracotta (highlights, key-word emphasis, accent strokes)  |
| `#1a1410` | Deep warm earth-brown — primary canvas, dark scrim                    |
| `#3c2e25` | Mid brown — secondary surface                                         |
| `#f5efe3` | Warm cream — outro title text, body on dark surface                   |
| `#a89884` | Mid taupe — secondary text                                            |
| `#3b7a57` | Deep tropical green — accent on plant/maker/source moments (rare use) |

Caption-on-image style: white body text + terracotta accent words in italic display serif. All caption text sits on a deep brown gradient scrim so it reads cleanly over varied b-roll.

## Typography

- **Display headlines** (60-130px): `Fraunces` weight 500-600 italic — serif with personality, artisan-craft feel
- **Captions / body** (38-58px): `Inter` weight 700 — bold sans for video legibility
- **Lower-third labels** (20-28px): `Inter` weight 500, letter-spacing 0.18em uppercase

## Motion

- Slow, breath-paced (1.0-1.5s entrance durations, between OA wellness and CC hip-hop energy)
- Easing: `power2.out` for organic entrances; `expo.out` for reveals; `sine.inOut` for breath/Ken Burns
- Staggers: 70-130ms between elements
- Image Ken Burns: subtle 1.0 → 1.08 scale over scene duration
- Scene transitions: crossfade or soft wipe — NEVER hard cuts, NEVER shader transitions with chromatic aberration

## What NOT to do

- No "natural" or "eco-friendly" as standalone words. Name the plant. Name the source. Name the maker.
- No clinical wellness jargon ("active ingredients", "efficacy", "actives") — UtamaSpice is sensory, not lab-coat.
- No medical or healing claims — never imply treatment.
- No aggressive eases (`back.out`, `elastic`, `bounce`) — breaks the ritual pacing.
- No staggers under 60ms — feels frantic.
- No pure black (`#000`) backgrounds — use `#1a1410` for warm depth.
- No em-dashes in any caption or title text — replace with periods, commas, colons (Jon-wide brand voice rule).
- Never reference the brand or products in beats 1-6; outro card only.
