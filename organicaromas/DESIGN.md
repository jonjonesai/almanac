# OrganicAromas — SP2 Video Visual Identity

## Style Prompt

Premium wellness meets curious neuroscience. Calm contemplation with scientific precision. Deep teal anchored on warm off-white canvas. Slow, breath-paced motion (1-2s entrances, never fast slams). Elegant serif headlines carry the wellness ritual feel; clean bold sans body carries the science. Subtle warmth keeps it human, not clinical. This is a brand for someone who buys a premium nebulizing diffuser because they believe scent shapes how they feel.

## Colors

| Hex       | Role                                                     |
| --------- | -------------------------------------------------------- |
| `#2eb0a4` | Primary — OA brand teal (highlights, key-word emphasis)  |
| `#1A202C` | Deep navy — primary text on light surface, dark canvas   |
| `#4A5568` | Mid slate — secondary text                               |
| `#FAFAF7` | Off-white — primary canvas                               |
| `#E8DCC4` | Warm cream — secondary surface, balances the cool teal   |
| `#D4A574` | Soft amber — accent on transformative moments (rare use) |

Caption-on-image style: text always appears on a soft black-shadow scrim or with thick text-shadow so it reads cleanly over varied b-roll backgrounds.

## Typography

- **Display headlines** (60-130px): `Playfair Display` weight 600 italic — elegant serif for wellness ritual feel
- **Captions / body** (38-56px): `Inter` weight 700 — bold sans for video legibility
- **Lower-third labels** (20-28px): `Inter` weight 500, letter-spacing 0.08em uppercase

## Motion

- Slow, breath-paced (1-2s entrance durations)
- Easing: `power2.out` for organic entrances; `expo.out` for moments of revelation; `power1.inOut` for ambient breath; `sine.inOut` for the slowest movements
- Staggers: 80-150ms between elements (slow, considered)
- Image Ken Burns: subtle 1.0 → 1.08 scale over scene duration, never more (avoids motion sickness)
- Scene transitions: crossfade or soft wipe (NEVER hard cuts, NEVER fast slams, NEVER shader effects with chromatic aberration — too caffeinated)

## What NOT to do

- No bright greens, lime, or saturated oranges that fight the teal
- No aggressive eases (`back.out`, `elastic`, `bounce`) — too caffeinated for wellness
- No staggers under 60ms — feels frantic
- No pure black (`#000`) backgrounds — use `#1A202C` for depth
- No full-screen linear gradients on dark backgrounds (H.264 banding)
- No hard cuts between scenes — always crossfade
- No more than 2 font families per composition
- **No em-dashes in any caption or title text** — replace with periods, commas, colons (Jon-wide brand voice rule)
